# Social And Video Workflow

## Per-Story Output

Every published story should generate:

- Web headline.
- SEO title.
- SEO description.
- beehiiv block.
- X post.
- Threads post.
- Instagram caption.
- Instagram title card prompt.
- TikTok/Reels script.
- Reporter voiceover script.
- Higgsfield prompt when video is worth generating.

## Video Format

Default short video:

- 20 to 45 seconds.
- 9:16 vertical.
- Reporter character on location or in a stylized desk setup.
- Fast setup, one useful fact, one joke, one source note.
- End card: `East Meets Nash - East Nashville, with receipts`.

## Higgsfield Prompt Shape

Use once reporter visual references exist:

```text
Vertical 9:16 local news short. [Reporter name], [consistent visual description], stands in an East Nashville-inspired setting: [Gallatin/Five Points/Shelby/etc.]. They deliver an expressive local news update with comedic timing. Polished but scrappy neighborhood media style, natural phone-camera energy, bright civic-parody color palette, no text on screen.
```

## Title Card Prompt Shape

```text
Create a bold editorial title card for East Meets Nash, an irreverent East Nashville local news brand. Use a parody civic-newspaper style with thick black rules, paper texture, tomato red, hot chicken gold, river blue, and park green. Headline: "[headline]". No fake logos besides East Meets Nash.
```

## Publishing Priority

Generate video first for:

- Big neighborhood outrage.
- Restaurant openings.
- Traffic and construction pain.
- Events with visual appeal.
- Civic explainers that need personality.
- Sponsor deliverables.

Do not spend Higgsfield credits on low-shareability filler.
