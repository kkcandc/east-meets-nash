## Opening Note

Welcome to East Meets Nash, a local news situation for people who live east of the river and have opinions about it.

We are here for the restaurant openings, the traffic pain, the patio drama, the permit files, the Facebook group archaeology, the things worth doing this weekend, and the tiny local mysteries that somehow become everyone's business by lunch.

No swearing. Plenty of receipts. Occasional side-eye. Deep affection. Let's begin.

## Issue Structure

1. Main Story
   - The biggest East Nashville item of the day.
   - Should include real source links and a named reporter voice.

2. The Quick Hits
   - 5 to 8 short items.
   - Restaurants, civic, events, development, family, gossip.

3. What People Are Mad About This Week
   - 3 to 5 community-chatter items.
   - Label each with source confidence.

4. Weekend Chaos Calendar
   - Events by day.
   - Include kid-friendly, food/drink, music, and free options.

5. Soft Launch Watch
   - Openings, closings, menus, pop-ups, hiring signs.

6. Permit File Says
   - One development/civic item translated from public records.

7. Classifieds
   - Starter listings or call for submissions.

8. Sponsor Slot
   - Founding sponsor offer until paid inventory exists.
