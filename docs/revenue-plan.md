# Revenue Plan

## Targets

- $5,000 total revenue by December 31, 2026.
- $20,000 monthly revenue within 12 months.

## Initial Revenue Products

1. Local sponsor placement
   - Price: `$100`
   - Appears in homepage feed, daily brief, or category block.
   - Self-serve checkout through ATXP credits.

2. Sponsored post
   - Price: `$1,000`
   - Clearly labeled.
   - Includes web post, newsletter inclusion, and social package.

3. Founding sponsor package
   - Suggested launch offer: `$1,500`
   - Includes 20 sponsor placements, 1 sponsored post, founding sponsor badge, weekly issue mention, and first right on category sponsorship.
   - Limit: 10 sponsors for launch scarcity.

4. Classifieds
   - Self-serve.
   - Suggested launch price: `$25` standard, `$50` featured.
   - Categories: housing, jobs, services, music gear, kid stuff, missed connections, garage sales, neighborhood pleas.

5. beehiiv ads
   - Use as baseline fill while local direct demand grows.

## Future Revenue Products

- Event tickets.
- Restaurant vouchers.
- Merch.
- Perks tier.
- Section sponsorships.
- Local deal drops.
- "Powered by" sponsorship on recurring columns.

## Sponsor Promise

Reach the people who know exactly which side of the river they are on and have opinions about it.

## Self-Serve Flow

1. Sponsor chooses placement type.
2. Sponsor uploads logo, headline, URL, and optional offer.
3. Sponsor logs in with ATXP.
4. Sponsor tops up if needed.
5. Sponsor pays with ATXP balance.
6. Placement goes live automatically or enters quick review if the creative is broken.

## Operating Principle

Do not overcomplicate inventory at launch. Sell simple, obvious placements first. Add segmentation and sponsorship tiers after there is traffic.
