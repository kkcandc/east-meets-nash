# Product Roadmap

## Launch Product

Public site:

- Homepage with top stories, quick hits, category filters, zone filters, search, events, restaurants, civic, gossip, classifieds, and sponsor slots.
- Article pages with source links, confidence labels, reporter bylines, reactions, comments, and related stories.
- Sponsor page with self-serve purchase flow.
- Tip email entry point.
- SEO-ready article structure.

Email:

- beehiiv daily morning brief.
- beehiiv weekly flagship issue.
- beehiiv ad inventory as an initial revenue layer.

Social:

- Instagram, TikTok, X, and Threads handles.
- Per-story social versions: short caption, thread, vertical video script, title card prompt, and reporter-specific version.

## Logged-In Product

ATXP account login:

- Use ATXP OIDC so users have one account across ATXP products.
- Store the ATXP `connection_token` as the session credential, following the ATXP Music pattern.
- Use ATXP balance for purchases and participation.

Personal feed:

- Readers pick zones, beats, reporters, restaurants, civic, gossip, events, family, development, nightlife, and classifieds.
- Feed learns from clicks, reactions, comments, saves, and hides.

Commerce:

- Buy classified ads.
- Buy restaurant vouchers.
- Buy event tickets.
- Buy merch.
- Sponsor a section or post.
- Top up ATXP balance without leaving the East Meets Nash context when Accounts supports direct payment links.

Community:

- Comments.
- Upvotes.
- Reactions.
- Saved stories.
- Tip submissions.
- "What people are mad about this week."

## Reaction Set

Launch with two obvious reactions so nobody has to decode the interface:

- ❤️ Love - the normal positive reaction.
- 👀 Side-Eye - the one local weird option for suspicious, messy, or "of course this happened" items.

Use other local reaction language as editorial flavor, stamps, or one-off campaign moments instead of default buttons.

## Future Product

- AI video desk with recurring reporter characters.
- Mobile app or PWA once repeat usage is proven.
- Paid perks tier with local deals, early event access, classifieds credits, sponsor discounts, and behind-the-scenes gossip digest.
- Neighborhood cloning system for future local brands, keeping East Meets Nash fully East Nashville.
